What We Do?

Providing Fresh Organic Produce and Clean Borehole Water Every Single Day

Planting

We produce fresh organic Fruits(Apples,Pomegranate,Strawberry Guava,Mangoes,Bananas,Pineapples) and Veggies(Spinach,Cabbages,Kales & Sukuma matumbo) among other produce.

Animal Keeping

At our farm, we keep milk goats,rabbits, chickens, bees and in future fish. We use their waste in our farm

Water Management

We use borehole water in our farm for irrigation, we also supply clean water to the community.
